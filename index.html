In the year CCXXI BC, after his brother Hasdrubal was assassinated, Hannibal Barca was put in command of the Carthaginian army.



Honoring a promise he made to his father, to never be a friend to Rome, Hannibal besieged the city of Saguntum in the year CCXIX BC.



Thus began the Second Punic War.
